About Us

Data Insight Solutions is a Uganda-based data consultancy firm committed to transforming data into actionable insights for smarter decision-making. We specialize in delivering high-quality services in Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E), data collection, dashboard development, and data analytics using industry-standard tools like Power BI, R, Python, SPSS, and STATA.

Founded with a vision to empower organizations across sectors—NGOs, government agencies, and private enterprises—our mission is to bridge the gap between data and impact. We believe that data is more than numbers; it’s the narrative behind every strategic decision, community outcome, and policy reform.

Our approach is collaborative, innovative, and impact-driven. We engage deeply with our clients to co-create tailored solutions, from designing real-time dashboards to conducting complex evaluations and training teams in cutting-edge analytics.
